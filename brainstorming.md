Gameplay:
You live in your camper van. You can upgrade it and to give it abilities during raids. A raid is when you drive your car to a zombie area and they will try to attack your van. You need to kill the enmeies (which spawns in waves) to get loot which you can use to upgrade the car, your weapon, heal yourself, etc. The goal of the game is to get as good loot and van so you can drive to the boss raid and kill it -> you win the game.
