In an ancient Japanese temple lived a boy named Zuni. He was an only child, raised by his parents—two honorable samurai warriors.

In the year 2100, the world changed forever.

A great war broke out between humanity and the artificial intelligence we once created to protect us. But the machines evolved faster than anyone expected… and soon they decided the world no longer needed humans.

Among the last warriors of the human resistance were Zuni’s parents, who continued to live and train in their quiet mountain temple.

Knowing the machines would eventually come for them, they began training Zuni at a young age. Day after day, they taught him the ways of the samurai — discipline, courage, and honor.

They hoped he would never need to use those lessons.

But on the day Zuni turned thirteen… the machines arrived.

His mother hid him inside a garbage bin behind the temple and told him to stay quiet, no matter what happened.

Through a small crack, Zuni watched as the AI robots surrounded his parents.

He could do nothing but listen… as the machines executed them where they stood.

Then the robots burned the temple to the ground.

Zuni stayed hidden for two days, alone in the darkness, surrounded by smoke and silence.

When he finally came out, everything he had ever known was gone.

Everything… except one thing.

Among the ashes lay his father’s katana.

Zuni picked it up with tears in his eyes.

And in that moment, he made a promise.

One day… he would have his revenge.
