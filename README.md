# KATANA

You are Zuni, a 23-year-old rogue samurai forged in the ashes of betrayal. Witnessing the brutal execution of your warrior parents by AI conquerors who destroyed your ancestral temple, you emerged from hiding with nothing but your father's sacred katana and an unbreakable vow of revenge. As the machines tighten their grip on a dystopian world scarred by the Great War of 2100, you roam as a nomadic avenger, striking from the shadows of your fortified van.

Navigate a shattered future Japan, confronting relentless waves of robotic foes in abandoned urban battlegrounds and colossal AI guardians in sacred temples. Wield your blade's razor edge, blending lightning-fast katana strikes, and tactical raids in a symphony of steel and circuits. Sharpen your katana, fortify your van, and push the boundaries of survival. Death is but a setback in your quest to dismantle the machine empire.
